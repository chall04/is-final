About Sun Valley

Sun Valley is a little known city found in central Idaho. Surrounded by the Smoky Mountain Range, Sun Valley provides residents and visitors with a place they can escape into nature. Sun Valley is predominately known for it's renowned skiing, but there are many activities that can be enjoyed in this unique place.
